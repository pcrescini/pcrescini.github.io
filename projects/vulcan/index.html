Vulcan

Vulcan is the leading direct to consumer brand for premium firearm accessories. By cutting out middlemen, leveraging social media, and utilizing global distribution networks we can provide innovative and premium products at affordable prices worldwide.

Vulcan aquired the help Cart.com's Growth Marketing team to design, develop, and deliver various media campaigns.

One of the campaigns required building out an email campaign landing page on Vulcan's Shopify storefront. As the front-end developer for this project with no prior Shopify experience, I had to familiarized myself Shopify's theme architecture and Liquid templating language.

Once I had a solid grasp, I created a new layout based on Vulcan's existing theme and then built out new custom sections following the Figma design created by our internal design team.
